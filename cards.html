A redesigned deck of playing cards

A little graphic design exercise I did to create a simplified deck of playing cards.

Source

Features

  • There are four simplified suits (heart, square, triangle, circle).
  • The deck uses Selenized for even contrast.
  • Each suit has its own colour rather than two sharing a colour.
  • The suit and the symbol are shown in the upper left corner, like a regular deck of cards.
  • The symbols are the digits 0-9 and the letters A and B to denote special cards.
  • The A and B cards use Selenized Black to emphasise the difference to other cards which use Selenized White.
