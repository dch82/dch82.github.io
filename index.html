About me

Hi there, I'm Danche. I am a student by day and tech guy at night. I like to play with the intersection of art and tech. I practice programming and have finalised in the Oxford University Computing Challenge. I also practice design, partcularly with icons, which you can find on my Figma profile. I have experience with Linux systems and Nix.
